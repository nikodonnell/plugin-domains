Domain settings (DNS records in GeekSpeak)


To connect your domain to Webflow, check out the Connecting a custom domain tutorial.

Connecting a custom domain

CNAME

CNAME record like WWW e.g www.example.com or blog.example.com

Webflow CNAME record values should be:

proxy-ssl.webflow.com.

If SSL is ON (recommended)

A records:

13.248.155.104 & 76.223.27.102

If SSL is OFF (not recommended)

A records:

23.235.33.229 & 104.156.81.229
